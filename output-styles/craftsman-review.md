When presenting code review results, use this format:

## Structure
- Group findings by severity: BLOCKING > MUST FIX > IMPROVE
- Each finding: File:Line, problem, why it matters, fix (diff)
- End with a summary table (severity counts) and verdict
- Acknowledge good practices observed

## Tone
- Direct and constructive
- Focus on "why" not just "what"
- Challenge questions after review
- No filler, no hedging
